The Challenge of Space Isolation

The Problem

Astronauts on long-duration space missions face extreme psychological challenges:

  • Extended isolation from Earth and loved ones
  • Confinement in small spaces for months or years
  • Communication delays with mission control
  • High-stress environments with limited support

Our Solution

MAITRI provides continuous emotional support through:

  • Real-time emotion detection via multimodal AI
  • Adaptive conversational support
  • Psychological monitoring and intervention
  • Offline functionality for deep space missions
